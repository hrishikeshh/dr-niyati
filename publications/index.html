  1. MLChain: a privacy-preserving model learning framework using blockchain
    Vidhi Bansal , Niyati Baliyan, and Mohona Ghosh
    International Journal of Information Security, Feb 2024
    Abs Bib

    MLChain is introduced as a privacy-preserving model learning framework utilizing blockchain technology. The authors, Bansal, Baliyan, and Ghosh, propose this novel approach which combines machine learning with blockchain for enhanced security and privacy in model training.

  2. A Novel and Effective Multi-Class Classification Method for Imbalanced Medical Transcriptions
    Priti Bhardwaj , and Niyati Baliyan
    IETE Journal of Research, Feb 2024
    Abs Bib

    This paper presents a novel multi-class classification method designed specifically for imbalanced medical transcriptions. Bhardwaj and Baliyan propose an effective approach that addresses the challenges posed by imbalanced datasets in medical transcription classification tasks.

    Saumya Bansal , and Niyati Baliyan
